What Does Random Look Like?

This is what it looks like.


I generated these 500x500 pixel images using a Python program I wrote on a fine January weekend. The program enumerates through each pixel in a desired image size and chooses a random RGB value (0-255) for each pixel. Pretty simple stuff. I used Pillow for image processing. I generated 20+ images and was absolutely enthralled by the results of the program. It was mesmerizing to witness all sorts of intricate patterns upon zooming in on the images. I spent an hour or so generating images to achieve certain patterns. The three images above are my favorite out of the collection of images.

That's all folks! These weekend projects will be bite-sized learning/interest projects I undertake for fun when I am not studying. Thank you for your time and I am wishing you a fantastic day!
